Balls of Doom

Game developed by Simon Cassista, website developed by Gabriel Côté

Made with Unity

Description

Ball that zooms across levels.

Controls

Use the WASD keys to move: W to jump, S to slow down, A to move left and D to move right. R is to restart and leave a body and the best part, T is for TURBO.
